Hackathon HackUSF 2026

BullSpace AI — USF Smart Library System

Built at HackUSF26 with team BombSquad-813. BullSpace AI solves a real problem: 50,000 USF students share one library with zero real-time visibility into seat availability. The app features a live occupancy dashboard across 6 floors, an interactive seat map with reservations, a study group finder, a tutoring hub with 14 real TAs, and an AI chat agent powered by Google ADK and Groq's llama-3.1-8b.
